#George Brown College

George Brown College is a well known college in Ontario,Canada which has many Campus.
Some of the campus include:
- Casa Loma campus which is a short 5-10 walk to the Casa Loma castle.
- Saint James campus which is located downtown close to king and union station easy ttc access and close walking range to any place downtown.
- Waterfront Campus: which is also locationed downtown and close to Saint James campus and a beautiful lake connected to it.
